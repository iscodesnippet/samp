There's a bit of chicken-and-egg here, because the company needs to both test
the impact of the repeated charge-and-discharge while also searching for the m
ost effective charging method. Because it wants the best battery, it starts wi
th something pretty robust

Mikolajczak and her team then checked up on the cells every 400 flights worth
of cycles. If the company wants to fly 10 or 12 missions per day, it isn't pra
ctical to replace the battery pack every few months, as some were found to req
uire. But, the testing showed that staying within the charging sweet spot prov
ided "substantially better performance," and some tested batteries could last
a full year.

Uber has more testing to do. It can refine its next tests further with the dat
a it's now getting — both from this first round of testing and from its manuf
acturing partners on what their vehicles will require — to continue to probe
the limits of the batteries, check additional flight cycles under different co
nditions and see how they perform in case of failures.

彭博資訊引述消息人士報導，蘋果正在為明年的iPhone研發屏下指紋辨識技術，蘋果和其

屏下指紋辨識器今年已成為安卓系統的標準配備，三星、華為、Oppo和小米的最新旗艦機
全都搭載這項技術。其魅力在於消除了顯示器周圍需要保留任何邊框的必要性，但又保留
了裝置正面的生物辨識功能。

知情人士表示，蘋果的屏下指紋辨識將會與現有的Face ID系統並存。如果測試成功，蘋
果考慮在2020年的iPhone機種中採用這種辨識器。據了解，供應商已證明有能力將此技術
整合進iPhone，但蘋果還未能成功量產。

蘋果2013年為iPhone導入指紋辨識功能，將其整合進Home鍵內。運用此技術的Touch ID系
統可用於解鎖裝置、核准支付和允許下載App，讓蘋果在速度和可靠性上掌握技術優勢。
這套系統在蘋果2017年推出iPhone X時，被採用臉部辨識技術的Face ID取代，又讓蘋果
再次在與對手的技術競爭中領先。

此外，據消息人士表示，蘋果也正在致力於推出自iPhone SE以來的首支平價iPhone，最
快可能在明年上半年推出。這支手機的外型將類似iPhone 8，採用4.7吋螢幕，將透過Hom
e鍵使用Touch ID，而非採用屏下辨識技術。
